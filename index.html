✅ Golden Rules
  • Per trade max 1-2% capital risk karo
  • SL hamesha lagao — kabhi nahi hatao
  • Risk:Reward minimum 1:2 rakho

🚫 Never Do This

  • FOMO mein entry mat lo
  • Tips pe blindly trade mat karo
  • 5 loss ke baad trading band karo
